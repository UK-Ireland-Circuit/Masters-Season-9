### **2. Format**

- Qualifiers:
    - 4 Open Qualifiers
    - Single Elimination BO1’s
    - Semi Finals BO3 for qualification
    - Top 2 teams qualify from each qualifier
- Group Stage:
    - GSL (2 Groups of 4 Teams) + Deciders
    - All Games BO3
    - 8 Teams Total
    - 4 Teams advance to Play-off
- Play-off:
    - Single Elimination BO3
    - 4 Teams Total
    - 2 Teams advance to LAN Final
- LAN Final:
    - Single Best of 5

### **3. Teams**

- 8 Teams from Open Qualifiers

### **4. Eligibility**

This tournament is solely for teams composing of at least 3/5 players that are citizens or residents of the following countries:

- Great Britain *(England, Scotland, Wales, Northern Ireland)*
- Ireland
- Isle of Man
- Jersey
- Guernsey

All other players must be playing from countries of the [EU Region](https://github.com/ValveSoftware/counter-strike_regional_standings/blob/main/model/util/region.js). 

### **5. Qualification**

Upon signing up to a qualifier, the five players selected as the starting lineup will be considered the Primary Team if the team qualifies.

If a team requires a stand-in for a qualifier, the stand-in must be registered as one of the team’s two substitutes and must not be listed among the starting five players.
